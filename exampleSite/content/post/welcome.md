## Features

The NanoBlogger theme brings the classic simplicity of the original NanoBlogger static site generator to Hugo.

- Clean two-column layout
- Sidebar with recent posts and categories
- Classic typography
- Easy to customize

Enjoy blogging with Hugo and this nostalgic theme!
